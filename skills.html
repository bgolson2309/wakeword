Sample Alexa Skills

Running Journal

Running Journal replaces old paper record keeping with Alexa & Running journal, and lets runners do the following:

  • Add running shoes (for tracking runs on)
  • Add running activity like date, distance, time, and how you felt
  • Get an email (if permission was activated) to see running activity that's been logged
  • Have fun with friends to ask Alexa Running journal, Who's the best running team?
Premium Access allows users to:
  • Get statistics like longest run, fastest run, distance logged on their current running shoes
  • Customize the name of the best running team to any team name

My Books

My Books has all the features to fully manage your favorite books to read, and supports functions like:

  • Adding a book to your reading list
  • Removing a book from your reading list
  • Seeing if a book is on your reading list
  • Deleting your entire reading list to start over
  • Displaying your reading list (On the Alexa Mobile Application)
  • Email your reading list
